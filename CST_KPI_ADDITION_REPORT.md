## Accuracy controls
- Missing source evidence is displayed as Data unavailable, not zero.
- Formal project participation requires explicit L'Harmoni/APRIL project evidence.
- The official L'Harmoni outcome rate remains withheld until the one-year timing rule is approved.
- Raw score interpretation remains withheld until the reporting owner approves the score-direction rule.
- Existing operational attendance, centre comparison, activity, CFS, gender, client, boundary and source-audit sections remain intact.
