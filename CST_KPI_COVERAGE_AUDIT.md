# CST KPI Coverage Audit

Source: `(15.9.2025) CST FY2025 Application Form_Tzuchi_final (1)(2).pdf`

## Project APRIL

- 1,000 seniors onboarded onto APRIL tools.
- 80% of APRIL-flagged at-risk seniors validated using staff assessment evidence (MMSE, GDS and/or SPPB as required by the application logic).
- 1,000 AAC clients reached annually.
- 100 volunteers reached annually.
- 200 caregivers reached annually.
- 3,000 AAC-client beneficiary instances across three years.
- 300 volunteer beneficiary instances across three years.
- 600 caregiver beneficiary instances across three years.
- 3,900 total beneficiary instances across three years.
- 100 complete MMSE/GDS/SPPB assessment sets annually.
- 300 unique seniors tracked across the three-year study period.
- Project implementation milestones and supporting evaluation measures.

## Project L'Harmoni

- 1,000 participating seniors in total.
- 500 participants at GLOW Bukit Batok.
- 500 participants at GLOW Nanyang.
- 60% of tracked seniors improve or maintain physical and/or cognitive wellbeing one year after joining.
- 100 complete MMSE/GDS/SPPB assessment sets annually.
- 300 unique seniors tracked across the three-year study period.
- Project implementation milestones.

## Accuracy controls

- Formal enrolment/outcome-study records are kept separate from ordinary attendance-derived operational figures.
- Missing source evidence remains `Data unavailable`; it is not replaced with zero or an estimate.
- The official L'Harmoni 60% outcome is withheld until the reporting team approves the follow-up timing window.
- Raw-score direction rules must be explicitly approved before raw MMSE/GDS/SPPB deltas are used.
- Source register, matched-field register and submission-readiness tables remain available for audit.
